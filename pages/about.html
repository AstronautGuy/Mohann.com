A World Of Refrigeration &
Processing Possibilities

It was in 1995 that a team of highly qualified engineers came together and envisioned the future needs of farmers and the food processing industry. Today, with a facility spread across 10,000 sq.ft., Mohann is one of India’s leading post-harvest & cold chain management companies.

A subsidiary of the ‘MOHANN GROUP OF COMPANIES’, established in 1960, we draw from our deep technological capabilities and market insights, to create a whole new world of sustainable food processing possibilities.

We have good credentials of working with Power, Oil & Gas, Diaries and Pharma, Food Processing and Water Treatment Industrial Sectors.

With over 46 years of cutting-edge technology and experience in the domain, we have built a reputation for providing reliable project solutions and dedicated service to a broad spectrum of clients across India and abroad as well.
